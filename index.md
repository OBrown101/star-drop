STAR DROP — PRIVACY POLICY (Effective July 2026)

Star Drop does not collect, store, or share any personal information itself. There are no accounts, and your scores are stored only on your device.

ADVERTISING: Star Drop displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID) and usage data to serve and measure ads, subject to Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy). On iOS, ad tracking occurs only if you allow it via the App Tracking Transparency prompt. In regions covered by GDPR, a consent form is shown and your choices are respected. Ads in Star Drop are configured to a G (general audiences) maximum content rating.

IN-APP PURCHASES: The optional "Remove Ads" purchase is processed entirely by Apple or Google; we never see your payment details.

CHILDREN: Star Drop is rated for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
